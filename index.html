About KS Numismatics

KS Numismatics LLC is a Canton, Michigan-based numismatic company dedicated to serving collectors, investors, and estates with honesty and expertise. Whether you're looking to grow your collection, liquidate an inherited estate, or invest in precious metals, our team brings years of experience and a passion for numismatics to every transaction.


We pride ourselves on fair pricing, transparent dealings, and personalized service for every customer — from first-time sellers to lifelong collectors.
